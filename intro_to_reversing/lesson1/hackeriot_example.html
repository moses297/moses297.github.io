What is Hackeriot?

Hackeriot is a practical, community-driven program blending software engineering with cybersecurity. Whether you’re aiming for your first role or sharpening your edge, you’ll leave with a portfolio and a network.

  • 8–12 weeks, part-time friendly
  • Weekly workshops + optional weekend labs
  • Capstone project with demo day

How it works

1) Onboard

Pick a track (Web, AppSec, or Blue Team) and meet your cohort.

2) Learn

Attend live workshops and complete bite-size challenges.

3) Build

Create tools and writeups—push everything to your portfolio.

4) Demo

Showcase your capstone to peers, mentors, and hiring partners.
